Fenugreek (Trigonella foenum-graecum)

Introduction

Fenugreek is an herb commonly used in cooking and traditional medicine. It has a distinctive maple-like aroma and is rich in health benefits.

Features

  • Scientific Name: Trigonella foenum-graecum
  • Family: Fabaceae
  • Uses: Culinary, medicinal, and cosmetic applications
  • Rich in fiber, protein, iron, and magnesium
  • Known for improving digestion and blood sugar levels

Health Benefits

Fenugreek is known for its numerous health benefits, including:

  • Regulating blood sugar levels
  • Boosting milk production in breastfeeding mothers
  • Reducing cholesterol levels
  • Promoting weight loss
  • Enhancing skin and hair health
