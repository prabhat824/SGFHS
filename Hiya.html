Betel Plant (Piper Betle)

The Betel Plant is a tropical vine known for its aromatic leaves, which are widely used in traditional medicine, cultural practices, and chewing with areca nut.

Scientific Classification:

  • Scientific Name: Piper betle
  • Family: Piperaceae (same family as black pepper)
  • Native to: South and Southeast Asia

Physical Characteristics:

  • A climbing vine with heart-shaped, glossy green leaves.
  • Leaves have a distinct spicy, slightly bitter taste.
  • The plant produces small, white, or greenish flowers but rarely bears fruit.

Uses & Benefits

1. Medicinal Uses

  • Improves digestion and stimulates appetite.
  • Has antibacterial and antifungal properties, helping in oral health.
  • Used in Ayurveda for treating coughs, colds, and headaches.
  • May help regulate blood sugar levels and relieve joint pain.
